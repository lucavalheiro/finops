​Cloudability Dashboards

The dashboards in Cloudability are customizable and easy to share with your stakeholders, developers and FinOps/IT Finance teams.

This is one, simple example that surfaces some valuable FinOps insights related to the company as a whole. Central to FinOps itself is the importance of enabling as many stakeholders as possible across a range of roles. An aspect of Cloudability that helps greatly in this sense is the filtered view system.
